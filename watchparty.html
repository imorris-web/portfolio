Digital Watch Party Concept for HBO Max

Details

June 2026

Concept, Visual Identity, Product Design

See the Case Study

Description

HBO Watch Party is an unofficial feature that lets you pair up with friends and family to watch tv shows, movies, or live sports events together. The goal of this project is to create a meaningful addition to HBO's streaming service and breathe new life into its branding.

The result is an immersive, virtual watchroom experience inspired by 90's aesthetic, movie theater interior trends, and the interactivity between friends during an at-home movie night. This project also showcases user journeys that explore the product's functionality, including a Mobile Chat integration for easier communication.
